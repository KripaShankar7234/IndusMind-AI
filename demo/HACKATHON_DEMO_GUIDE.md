# ET AI Hackathon 2.0 - Demo Script & Guide

## Demonstration Walkthrough Steps

1. **Landing Page Overview (`/`)**
   - Highlight the animated background glow, ROI stats, and enterprise positioning.
   - Point out multi-format ingestion support and SOC2 security architecture.

2. **Organization Registration & Sign In (`/register`, `/login`)**
   - Log in as Dr. Sarah Jenkins (Chief Reliability Engineer).
   - Demonstrate single sign-on (Azure AD / Okta SAML) integration options.

3. **Operations Dashboard (`/app/dashboard`)**
   - Review 5 live KPI cards (Total Documents, Equipment Count, AI Queries, Compliance %, Recent Uploads).
   - Interactive Recharts graphs: Ingested Documents over time, Facility Compliance progress bar, AI query volume line chart.

4. **Document Library & Drag-and-Drop Ingestion (`/app/documents`)**
   - Drag & drop OEM manuals (PDF, DOCX, Images).
   - Inspect status pills and click the Eye icon to view embedded vector chunk excerpts.

5. **Copilot AI Assistant (`/app/assistant`)**
   - Ask industrial prompt: *"What is the OEM bearing vibration limit for GE 9HA Gas Turbine?"*
   - Observe typing animation, grounded answer, 98% confidence score badge, and Source Citation cards with exact page references.

6. **Predictive Maintenance Intelligence (`/app/maintenance`)**
   - Click equipment cards to inspect Inferred Possible Causes, OEM recommendations, historical failure resolution timelines, and PM checklists.

7. **Regulatory Compliance Checker (`/app/compliance`)**
   - Audit SOPs against ISO 45001 & OSHA Title 29.
   - Review compliance dial, active safety gap cards, and AI corrective action recommendations.

8. **Executive PDF Report Generator (`/app/reports`)**
   - Select report type (Root Cause Analysis / Maintenance / Audit Summary).
   - Click "Generate Enterprise Report" and trigger instant PDF print export.
